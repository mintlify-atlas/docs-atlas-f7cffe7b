## How BidAuc works

BidAuc operates on a simple, two-sided marketplace model connecting **Seekers** (those who need work done) with **Providers** (skilled professionals offering services).

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Register with your phone number using OTP verification. Choose your role as a Seeker or Provider.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Post or browse jobs">
    Seekers post jobs with details, images, and urgency. Providers browse available jobs filtered by category.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Place or receive bids">
    Providers submit competitive bids with their price and description. Seekers review all bids on their job.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Hire and complete">
    Seekers accept the best bid, the Provider delivers the service, and the Seeker marks the job as complete.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key features

- **OTP-based authentication** — Secure phone number verification for all users
- **Dual-role system** — Users register as Seekers (job posters) or Providers (service professionals)
- **Competitive bidding** — Providers compete on price and quality, giving Seekers the best deal
- **Job lifecycle tracking** — Track jobs through pending, active, completed, and canceled states
- **Profile management** — Build trust with detailed profiles and profile pictures
- **Dashboard views** — Tailored dashboards for Seekers and Providers with filtered job lists

<Note>
  BidAuc uses cookie-based JWT authentication. All protected endpoints require an active session established through OTP login.
</Note>
